About Consumer Staples

The Consumer Staples sector consists of companies that provide essential products that people use on a daily basis, regardless of their financial situation. These include food, beverages, household goods, and hygiene products. Because demand for these products remains relatively stable even during economic downturns, Consumer Staples stocks are often considered defensive investments.
