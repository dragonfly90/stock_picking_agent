Healthcare & Pharma Sector

The healthcare sector spans the full continuum of medicine: Large-cap pharma (blockbuster drugs, pipelines), Biotech (high-risk/high-reward innovation), Medical devices (surgical tools, diagnostics hardware), Managed care (health insurance, PBMs), and Life sciences tools (instruments, CROs, CDMOs). Healthcare is defensive — demand is largely non-discretionary — but faces drug pricing pressure, patent cliffs, and regulatory risk.
